Frequently Asked Questions

Welcome to Our FAQ Page

Here you will find answers to the most common questions we receive. If you can't find what you're looking for, please contact us.

Contact Us More FAQs

What age groups can join the volleyball club?

Our volleyball club is open to teenagers aged 12-18 years old.

Do I need prior volleyball experience to join?

No prior experience is necessary. We welcome beginners as well as experienced players.

When and where are the practices held?

Practices are held every Tuesday and Thursday from 4 PM to 6 PM at the La Jolla High School gymnasium.

What equipment do I need to bring?

Players should wear comfortable athletic clothing, non-marking court shoes, and bring a water bottle. Knee pads are recommended but not required.

Are there any membership fees?

Yes, there is a monthly membership fee of $50, which covers the cost of facilities, coaching, and equipment maintenance.

Do you participate in tournaments?

Yes, our club participates in local and regional tournaments throughout the year. Participation is optional but encouraged for skill development and team bonding.

How can I register for the club?

You can register by filling out the registration form on our website or by visiting us during practice hours for a paper registration form.
